Professional Background

I'm Parker Hewett, a graduate of the University of Oklahoma's Price College of Business with a Bachelor's in Management Information Systems (MIS) and a Master of Science in Business Analytics. I recently completed my master's degree in December 2025, where I refined my skills in Python, SQL, data visualization, and process optimization.

Currently, I work as an Engineering Technician (Environmental Data Systems) at Williams Companies in Tulsa, OK. My day-to-day work focuses on managing and configuring PI Systems, maintaining dynamic reports using PI DataLink and SSRS, and facilitating data transfer into Databricks for advanced analytics. I thrive in environments where I can combine technical expertise with strategic thinking to improve processes, enhance data accessibility, and drive operational efficiency.
