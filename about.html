Intro

My name is Parker Hewett, and I am a graduate of the University of Oklahoma’s Price College of Business with a Bachelor’s in Management Information Systems (MIS). I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics, where I continue to refine my skills in Python, SQL, data visualization, and process optimization.

I have gained valuable experience through internships and leadership roles. As an Industrial Engineer Intern at SageNet, I developed a company-wide data catalog in SharePoint, improving information accessibility. I also worked with SAP and ServiceNow for asset tracking and contributed to inventory and procurement processes. Additionally, as an IT Intern at NGL Energy Partners, I collaborated with Network and Cybersecurity teams, configured hardware, and optimized network performance.

Beyond my technical experience, I have a strong foundation in leadership and financial management. As Treasurer of Phi Delta Theta, I managed a $200K annual budget, implemented financial tracking systems, and communicated financial updates to alumni and members. I also earned the rank of Eagle Scout, leading service projects and mentoring younger scouts.

In addition to my professional and leadership experiences, I am a freelance photographer, planning and executing photoshoots while delivering high-quality, professionally edited images

I am passionate about leveraging technology and analytics to drive business value and solve complex problems. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out to connect!
